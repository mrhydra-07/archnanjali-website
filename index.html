Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer home delivery for electronics and hardware?

Yes, we offer home delivery for all our products within the local Madhubani area. Delivery charges may apply based on distance and order size. Please contact us for details.

What is the booking process for the marriage hall?

To book our hall, please contact us to check for availability on your desired date. A 50% advance payment is required to confirm the booking, with the remaining balance due one week before the event.

Do you provide warranties on electronic items?

Absolutely. All electronic products come with the official brand warranty. We are an authorized dealer for all brands we carry, and we will assist you with any warranty claims.
